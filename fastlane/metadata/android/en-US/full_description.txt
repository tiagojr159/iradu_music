Iradu Music is a fork of Iradu Music. It comes with all the privacy features of Iradu Music, but also adds features from SponsorBlock and ReturnYouTubeDislike. Like Iradu Music, it does not use any Google framework libraries, or the YouTube API. It only parses the website in order to gain the information it needs. Therefore this app can be used on devices without Google Services installed. Also, you don't need a YouTube account to use Iradu Music, and it's FLOSS.
